## Internal linking plan

- Homepage links to all format pages, trust pages, and articles.
- Every format page links back to the homepage tool and to the `why remove` and `how to remove location` articles.
- Every article links back to the homepage tool and at least one format page.
- Footer links to `About`, `Privacy`, and `FAQ`.

## Content principles

- Keep the homepage conversion-first with the tool above the fold.
- Use article pages to support long-tail discovery and funnel users back to the tool.
- Keep claims precise: browser-only, image-only, no fake counters, no fake reviews.
